About The Book

The Paranormal Adventures of Samuel Wiley takes place after one destined bus ride where Samuel meets an enigmatic lady who drops a letter addressed to him as she disembarks. The contents of the letter changes his life, reality, and unlocks forgotten secrets buried long ago.

About Ali

Born on September 20th 1990, in Portland, Oregon, Ali Saidi is not your average writer. Ali is a geek, he's been conflicted about whether he prefers DC over Marvel ever since he was a child. Ali is very interested in paranormal sightings such as UFOs, Ghosts, the Mothman, and of course the Chupakabra. And his favorite time of year is Halloween. The Paranormal Adventures of Samuel Wiley has been in the making for over 10 years, and promises to blow your mind away just as Samuel was thrown into an unimaginable realm where his whole world irrevocably changed.
